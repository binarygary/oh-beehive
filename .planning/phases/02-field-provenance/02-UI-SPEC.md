## Provenance Interaction Contract

- Apply provenance only to structured fields populated by the parser result in `create` and `edit`.
- Show provenance on every parser-owned structured field, including booleans, enums, numeric scores, counts, text inputs, and disease checkboxes.
- Never show provenance on `rawNotes`, `hiveId`, `inspectedAt`, or the follow-up questions alert.
- Render the indicator at label level: label text followed by a `badge badge-sm badge-primary` with text `AI`.
- Keep the field control itself visually neutral by default. Do not add heavy fills, glows, or high-contrast outlines.
- If an additional parsed-state treatment is used, limit it to a subtle label tint only; no persistent full-input accent border.
- Clear provenance for one field immediately after the keeper changes that field manually.
- Reset all provenance before each new parse, then repopulate only from fields returned in that parse response.
- Fields omitted by the parser remain unmarked.
- Provenance must update on the same Livewire round-trip as the field change; no Alpine or client-side JS layer.
